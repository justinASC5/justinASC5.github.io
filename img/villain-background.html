This question goes under Batman Movies, so I suppose well stick to the movies then. Despite the fact that there are some epic comic book fight sequences out there.

I wont be including animated movies because I feel like the difference between animation and live-action is just too significant to properly judge between the two.

Im also majorly limiting this to hand-to-hand combat scenes.

5. The Dark Knight, first Batman appearance.

Its nicely choreographed, it has a smooth pacing, its not too intense - it makes the fight seem more real and more like a day-to-day fight for Batman. This is in contrast to the others on this list who are often quite intense, which is not a bad thing - just not whats needed here.

4. Drug bust

So this ones epic. Its the first we see of Batman on the streets. Nolan totally nails the Batman feel of stealth and strength and thats really nice to see here.

3. Batman vs Superman.

So the movie had its flaws, this scene is still totally epic and really shows the entirety of Batmans awesomeness. I like it a lot.

2. Hong-Kong fight scene.

Again, this scene is beautifully choreographed, gripping, intense and still maintains the stealth aspect of Batman. Really very quite nice.

1. Warehouse scene.

It is breathtakingly wonderful. Some will disagree, I dont care. Its genuinely a wonderful scene that fully shows the way Batman deals with innumerable thugs. It is the best Ive ever seen. I couldnt oversell it if I tried.

Bonus:

Just because its cool.

Enjoy.
